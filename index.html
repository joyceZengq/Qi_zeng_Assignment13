Discoverability

The user should be able to determine what actions are possible and the state of the device.

Mapping

Relationship between the controls and their movements and the results in the world.

Conceptual Models

The user should be able to determine what actions are possible and the state of the device.

Constraints

Constraints restrict the kind of interactions that can take place.

Feedback

Sending back to the user information about what action has actually been done, what result has been accomplished.

Affordances

The perceived and actual properties of an object. Primarily the fundamental properties that people recognize and use to determine just how the thing could possibly be used.

Signifiers

Any mark or sound, any perceivable indicator, that communicates appropriate behavior to a person. Use your 5 senses. They signal what actions are possible and how they should be done.
